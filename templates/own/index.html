Introduction

The Technex Campus Ambassador Program is a platform for students who are passionate about technology, creativity and innovation to spread the charm of one of the oldest techno-management festival of this country in their college. A Technex Campus Ambassador is a leader with a keen interest in starting and building community of people equally passionate and ready to show off their dormant skills to the world.

You will be the face, voice, and evangelist of Technex at your campus. You will be the champion of our vision for hosting great technically innovative contests and building one of the largest network of enthusiasts like you all over the country.
